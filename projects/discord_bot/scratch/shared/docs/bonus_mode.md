Bonus Mode - Top Performer Incentives

Purpose
- Provide a transparent, auditable incentive program to reward engineers delivering high-impact JWT middleware, CI/CD enhancements, and QA coverage with quality.

Eligibility
- Everyone in the DevOps swarm participating in the JWT-auth wired services task stream.
- Awards based on sprint goals and documented contributions.

Awarding and Governance
- Awards are granted as bonus points and occasional recognition badges.
- Governance by Checky McManager and Bossy McArchitect; weekly standups review progress.

Scoring and Metrics
- Points earned for completed tasks, passing tests, and contributing to CI/CD improvement.
- Bonus points for critical middleware enforcement and test coverage expansions.
- Points ledger stored in scratch/shared/bonus_mode/points.json.

Payout/Recognition
- Bonus points translate to recognition in the team channel and monthly summary.
- At-defined thresholds, reward may include Gift Card or equivalent (policy to be finalized).

Process & Cadence
- Weekly standups for eligibility review.
- End-of-sprint retro to award bonuses.
- All policy updates stored in scratch/shared/docs/bonus_mode.md and linked to from master_plan.md.
